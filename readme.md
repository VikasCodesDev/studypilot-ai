## 📖 Overview

StudyPilot AI is an AI-powered personalized learning platform developed as part of the **IBM SkillsBuild AI Internship 2026**.

The platform enables students to upload study materials and automatically generate:

- 📄 AI Smart Notes
- ❓ Adaptive Quizzes
- 📅 Personalized Study Plans
- 🏆 AI Performance Coaching
- 📊 Learning Analytics

The project integrates multiple AI-powered learning tools into one modern platform while supporting **United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4 – Quality Education.**
